What Are Transactional Emails?

Transactional emails are automated emails that are triggered by a specific user action on your website or application. Unlike promotional or marketing emails, transactional emails are expected by the user and contain essential information. These emails play a critical role in building trust, ensuring smooth communication, and improving customer experience.

For example, when a customer places an order on UrbanSteps, they expect an order confirmation email immediately. When they request a password reset, they expect a secure reset link within seconds. Transactional emails ensure that these important communications happen instantly and reliably. Because they are directly related to user activity, transactional emails usually have very high open rates.

Our system is built with advanced email infrastructure to ensure fast delivery, maximum security, and excellent inbox placement. We prioritize reliability because transactional emails are not optional — they are essential to your business operations.
