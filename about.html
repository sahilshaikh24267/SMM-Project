About UrbanSteps

Who We Are

UrbanSteps is a premium footwear brand dedicated to delivering stylish, comfortable and performance-driven sneakers for modern lifestyles. Founded with the vision of redefining everyday footwear, we blend innovation with fashion to create shoes that inspire confidence.

Our Journey

🚀 2022 – The Beginning

UrbanSteps started as a small online sneaker store with a passion for quality footwear.

📦 2023 – Growing Community

We expanded across India and served 10,000+ happy customers nationwide.

🌎 2025 – Brand Expansion

Launched affiliate program and premium performance collections.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

Deliver high-quality footwear combining comfort, durability and trend-setting design.

🌟 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted sneaker brand focusing on innovation and satisfaction.
